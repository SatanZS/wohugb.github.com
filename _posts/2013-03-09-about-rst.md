让Gedit支持rst
===============

    $cd /usr/share//usr/share/gtksourceview-3.0/language-specs

    $sudo wget https://github.com/marianoguerra/rst2html5/blob/master/data/gtksourceview-3.0/rst.lang

    重启Gedit
